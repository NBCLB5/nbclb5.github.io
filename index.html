Web Developer

I am a continuous learner with an aspiration for web development. I am in search of a team where I can grow and sharpen my skills with guidance from a mentor or a team of experts. Through past experiences I have had the opportunity to contribute to three overseas teams as well as four government agencies. I have also completed several courses and obtained a few certificates, including FreeCodeCamp responsive web design and AWS cloud practitioner. You can check out some of my work here. If you are a part of a team that sounds like the description above or who is someone who knows of such a team, please feel free to reach out to me.

Do you want to know a little bit more about me? Well, I enjoy a good cup of coffee and I love being outdoors and seeing the stars or the city light. I feel at home around water whether it is by a river, a lake, or the ocean. I have lived aboard for close to ten years and have been to six of the seven continents. One dream of mine is to have a hobby farm and do some beekeeping one day.

Every day, I try to live my life as positive and authentic as I can. I work hard to play hard with the philosophy of you work to live not live to work. If you have any questions for me or would like to talk about a project, you can find my contact links below. I look forward to working with you.
